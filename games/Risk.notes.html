This scenario uses the map from the classic game of Risk. Alliance A is Asia(Chinese), Africa(Italians) and South America (Japanese). Alliance B is North America(Americans), Europe (Germans) and Australia (British). There is bonus for complete control of your starting continent.
South America-2
Australia-2
Africa-3
Europe-5
North America-6
Asia-7
There are no sea zones. Units are identitical to Minimap. Unlimited production in your capital factory.
